1. Mercedes-Benz 300SL Gullwing
Considered the gold standard at the time, the Mercedes-Benz 300SL Gullwing has all that a beautiful car needs. The body is simple but beautifully luxurious and sporty, most certainly the "wings" of the car. 2. Jaguar XKSS
Another one from Jaguar, but here is the XKSS. Jaguar has been known for its original car designs, from the early models such as the XK120, XK140 to the golden age of the E-Type and later the F-Type. For the XKSS, Jaguar also makes a sports car but more sophisticated, more elegant E-Type, is one of the favorite cars of the "cool king" Steve McQueen

3. Ferrari 250 GTO
There are only 39 Ferrari 250 GTOs in the world, and so the car has been named "the hottest car of all time". Its beauty is indescribable, with a soft and flexible body design with curves. The design is also being tested in the wind tunnel at the University of Pisa in a very strict way, so that the car is not only beautiful but also works on the road in the best way.
